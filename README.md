# 📊 Global Super Store Analysis  
This project provides a **detailed analysis** of **Global Super Store's** sales and profitability from **2011 to 2014** using **Power BI, DAX, and M Language**. The dashboard offers key insights into sales trends, customer behavior, product profitability, and regional performance.

---

## 🚀 **Project Overview**  
- **Total Sales**: £12.6M  
- **Profit**: £1.5M  
- **Top Customer**: Tom Ashbrook (£35.7K revenue)  
- **Most Profitable Product**: Canon imageCLASS 2200 (£24K)  
- **Most Sold Product**: Sanford Pencil Sharpener, Easy-Erase (849 units)  
- **Top Revenue Region**: West & Central  
- **Top Revenue Country**: United States (£2.3M)  
- **Data Source**: Kaggle (51,290 rows, 24 columns)  

---

## 📊 **Key Insights & Visualizations**  
✅ **Sales Trends**  
- Peak months: **November, December, September, June**  
- Low months: **January, February, July, October**  
- **Year-over-year growth observed**  

✅ **Customer & Product Insights**  
- Consumer segment leads revenue **(£6.5M)**
- The **Binder** category has the highest orders, while **Paper** drives the most profit  
- **Violin plot** used for profit distribution across categories  

✅ **Geographical Insights**  
- **Map visualization** shows the USA as the top revenue contributor  
- **Bubble chart** highlights sales hotspots in North America, Europe, and Asia-Pacific  

✅ **Profitability Performance**  
- **Gauge chart** compares actual sales (£13M) vs. target (£14M)  
- **Bar chart** ranks cities by revenue (New York City leads)  
- **Table visualization** highlights high-margin countries (China 21.51%, India 21.36%)  

---

## 🛠 **Technologies Used**  
- **Power BI** for data visualization  
- **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** for calculated measures  
- **M Language** for data transformations  
- **SQL & Power Query** for data preprocessing
